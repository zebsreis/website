intro
In the game for around 5 years, I’m a digital designer and Webflow developer from Portugal, currently based in Bali. I work independently, crafting expressive and functional digital experiences, from concept to launch. Passionate about turning ideas, visuals, and storytelling into thoughtful, memorable creations. I love collaborating with studios, artists, and makers who value thoughtful design and creative exploration.
tools
I primarily design in Figma and develop in Webflow (currently favoring the Lumos framework). My approach stays flexible, regularly adapting to each brief’s unique challenges, whether through custom coding, specialized libraries, performance optimization tools, or the latest AI-driven solutions. I continually evolve my toolkit to thoughtfully align with every creative vision and goal.
services
Design: I design brand identities and websites that are both expressive and intentional. Whether it's crafting a visual language or mapping out an interface, I focus on clarity, emotion, and cohesion across every element.

Development: I build custom websites and interactive virtual experiences, often with a strong emphasis on animation and fluidity. I prefer to be involved from the ground up, so the development fully aligns with the design vision.

Direction: I guide creative projects with a sharp eye for detail and consistency. From concept to execution, I help shape ideas into cohesive, memorable digital experiences that feel both thoughtful and alive.
