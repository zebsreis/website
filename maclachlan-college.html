MacLachlan College

2024
MacLachlan College is a forward-thinking educational institution known for its rigorous academics and real-world learning experiences, supported by state-of-the-art facilities.
Aimed to extend its forward-thinking educational approach into the digital space, offering prospective students and families a way to explore the campus remotely. The goal was to create a virtual tour that didn't just showcase the physical facilities, but also communicated the school’s progressive values and vibrant academic environment. The strategy focused on creating a tool that felt intuitive, immersive, and aligned with the school’s emphasis on innovation and experiential learning.

The result was a fully interactive 360° virtual tour built to guide users through key areas of the campus—from classrooms and labs to community spaces—allowing them to experience the environment at their own pace. Clear navigation, layered hotspots, and contextual information help demystify the school’s offerings and create a more personal connection. The tour was designed to be device-responsive and user-friendly, serving as a practical recruitment asset for both local and international audiences.
